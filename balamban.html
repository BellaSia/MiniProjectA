Balamban Flower Garden

Bright red and yellow blooms, A sea of pinks, A windmill, Mountains in the distance, which the owners call the Little Amsterdam of Cebu.

Sirao Garden, though, is best known for its celosias, which look like bright flames, usually in red and yellow. Its name in Greek actually means “burning.” Long before other flower gardens opened and touted celosias as attractions, Sirao Garden already had these fiery blooms and became famous for them.




How to get there:

Brgy. Gaas, Balamban, Cebu – few meters away from Adventure Cafe (30-45 min drive from JY square or Marco Polo via the scenic Transcentral highway).

75 pesos per adult, 50 pesos per head for Kids, PWD & Senior Citizen

Make sure you follow and respect the garden’s rules.
