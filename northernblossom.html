Northern Blossom Flower

Northern Blossom Flower Farm is a two-hectare farm with mountain views located in Atok, Benguet. It features echeveria plants, colorful rose cabbages, vibrant sunflowers, pastel-colored flowers, marigolds, delphinium, Matthiola, and roses planted in wide rolling fields.




How to get there:

From Baguio City, ride a bus bound to Atok, Benguet, and drop off in front of Northern Blossom or the Atok municipal hall

Use the Waze app and pin the Northern Blossom Flower Farm or Saint Paul’s Academy of Sayangan in Atok, Benguet.

250 pesos good for two hours which includes a coffee and bread. On the other hand, toddlers and children below 5 years old have no entrance fee.

The best month to visit the farm is from December to March when the roses and sunflowers are in full bloom.
